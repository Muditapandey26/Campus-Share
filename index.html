The Campus Challenge

Academic materials shouldn't cost a fortune.

Every semester, thousands of rupees are spent on brand-new reference books, lab gear, and study modules that get used for a few months and then sit on a shelf collecting dust. CampusShare breaks this cycle by connecting students directly.

High Cost of Textbooks

Retail prices for engineering and reference books add unnecessary financial stress every semester.

Accumulating Resource Waste

Perfectly good lab coats, component kits, and study guides are thrown away instead of being reused.
